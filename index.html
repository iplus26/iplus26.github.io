About Me

Front end developer, an undergraduate of UESTC (short for University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, I still can't spell its English full name), living in Chengdu right now.

I am developer of Watched, a Windows 8.1 app for Douban Movie (Chinese version IMDb), and the author of doumail-back, a Safari extension (which gains me most stars on GitHub, and honestly I don't want them... on such a repository).

I spend major of my time on front end development now, but I am also able and willing to do some back end development and other works.

Front End Dev

I write JavaScript since this February. JavaScript is a powerful tool that easily make my ideas come true. I started from a simple Safari extension, dbmovie, which shows IMDb ratings and more in Douban Movie page. There were several implements for Chrome or Firefox, but no Safari version.

Then I made Watched, a Windows 8.1 app for Douban Movie. I like the Modern UI design, and Douban Movie. Kill two birds with one stone! Watched once ranked #2 in Windows Store New Rising Apps, and I was so excited, and then I never update it...

CSS is also amazing. I used to dream to be an artist one day. With CSS, I can do a huge number of things that I couldn't do with nearly no PS skills. I am very interested in different implements using CSS right now.
